# Legal Source And Compliance

## Official Source Workflow

Browse or otherwise verify official sources when the copy touches:

- law names, elements of crime, protected interests,
- penalties, administrative sanctions, license/discipline consequences,
- recently amended areas,
- procedure such as warrants, detention, temporary measures, administrative appeals,
- victim protection or reporting routes.

Preferred query patterns:

- `site:law.go.kr <법률명> <핵심 조문 또는 용어>`
- `site:easylaw.go.kr <분야명> <생활법령 키워드>`
- `<정부기관명> <절차명> 공식`

Use official sources for facts, then write in plain Korean. Do not paste long statutory language into marketing copy.

## Safe Legal Wording

Prefer:

- `문제 될 수 있습니다`
- `검토해야 합니다`
- `사안에 따라 달라집니다`
- `자료를 보존해야 합니다`
- `조사 전 진술 방향을 정리해야 합니다`
- `형사절차와 행정절차를 함께 살펴야 합니다`

Avoid:

- `무조건`, `반드시 이깁니다`, `확실히`, `100%`
- `불기소 가능`, `무혐의 가능` as promotional prediction
- `최고`, `유일`, `No.1`, `압도적`
- `무료 상담`, `저렴한 수임료`
- `검찰 인맥`, `전관예우`, `내부 사정`
- `처벌을 피할 수 있습니다`

## Lawyer Advertising Guard

Before finalizing user-facing copy, check `docs/00-compliance/lawyer-ad-regulations.md`.

High-risk areas:

- Result guarantees and predictions
- Success-rate or case-count claims
- Unsupported “전문” phrasing
- Free/discount claims
- Competitor comparisons
- Sensational or dignity-harming phrasing, especially for sex-crime and victim content

## Final Answer Source Note

If web verification was used, include concise source links in the final answer. Say what they were used for, for example:

- “법률 용어와 분류 확인: 국가법령정보센터 / 찾기쉬운 생활법령정보”
- “구체 처분 가능성은 사건별로 달라 변호사 검수 전 단정하지 않았습니다.”
